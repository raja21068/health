Natural Skincare Products

Nourish Your Skin Naturally

Moisturizers

  • Aloe Vera Gel: Hydrates, soothes, and reduces redness.
  • Shea Butter: Rich in vitamins A and E, great for deep hydration.
  • Jojoba Oil: Mimics skin's natural oils and is great for sensitive skin.

How to Use: Apply a small amount to clean skin, gently massaging until absorbed. Ideal for daily use, especially after cleansing or as a night-time treatment.

Cleansers

  • Coconut Oil: Effectively removes makeup and impurities.
  • Honey: Antibacterial and moisturizing; ideal for sensitive skin.
  • Apple Cider Vinegar: Used as a toner to balance skin’s pH.

How to Use: Use a small amount and gently massage onto damp skin, then rinse. For toners, apply with a cotton pad after cleansing.

Exfoliants

  • Oatmeal: Gentle enough for sensitive skin and helps reduce irritation.
  • Sugar and Honey Scrub: Smoothes and moisturizes.
  • Coffee Grounds: Reduces cellulite and exfoliates dead skin.

How to Use: Apply a small amount to damp skin, massaging in circular motions. Use 1-2 times a week to avoid over-exfoliation.

Face Masks

  • Turmeric and Yogurt: Brightens skin and reduces inflammation.
  • Clay Masks (Bentonite or Kaolin): Draws out impurities and minimizes pores.
  • Avocado and Honey: Hydrates deeply and adds a glow.

How to Use: Apply an even layer to clean, dry skin and leave on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse with lukewarm water. Use 1-2 times per week.

Serums and Oils

  • Rosehip Oil: High in vitamins C and A, good for scars and anti-aging.
  • Tea Tree Oil: Naturally antibacterial, great for acne-prone skin.
  • Argan Oil: Lightweight and rich in antioxidants for dry skin.

How to Use: Apply a few drops to clean skin, gently pressing into the skin. Ideal for use in the morning or night after moisturizer.
