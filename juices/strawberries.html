Below are two simple and delicious strawberry smoothie methods perfect for breakfast or an afternoon treat.

Method 1: Syrupy Strawberry Shake

  • 1 cup strawberry
  • 2 tsb sugar
  • 1 tsb Pashto (likely a nut paste or regional flavoring)
  • 1 tsb strawberry syrup
  • 200ml milk

Instruction: Blend all ingredients together until smooth. Serve chilled.

Method 2: Classic Strawberry Milkshake

  • 100g strawberry
  • 2 tsb sugar
  • Ice (as needed)
  • 200ml milk

Instruction: Blend strawberries, sugar, and ice with milk until frothy. Serve cold.

Method 3: Strawberry Banana Smoothie

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 150ml yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (as needed)

Instruction: Blend strawberries, banana, yogurt, and ice until creamy. Add honey for extra sweetness. Perfect for breakfast!

Method 4: Strawberry Oat Energy Shake

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 3 tbsp rolled oats
  • 200ml almond milk (or any milk)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or honey

Instruction: Blend strawberries, oats, and milk until smooth. Stir in chia seeds. A fiber-rich smoothie that keeps you full longer.

Tips for Smoothie Perfection

  • Use frozen strawberries for a thicker texture
  • Add yogurt for extra creaminess and probiotics
  • Sweeten with honey or dates if preferred
  • Garnish with sliced strawberries or mint
