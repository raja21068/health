Coffee Smoothie Recipes

Get your caffeine fix in a cool and refreshing way with these easy coffee smoothie recipes. Whether you like it chilled, hot, or blended with chocolate, there’s a version here to energize your day.


Start with the whipped base and explore three creative ways to enjoy coffee as a smoothie!

Whipped Coffee Base

A frothy foundation for any cold coffee smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsb coffee
  • 2 tsb sugar
  • 2 tsb water

Instructions

  1. Whisk all ingredients together until frothy
  2. Use as a topping or stir into cold milk

Iced Coffee Smoothie

Simple and classic—just pour over ice and enjoy!

Steps

  1. Add ice cubes to a glass
  2. Pour in milk
  3. Top with the whipped coffee base

Hot Coffee Smoothie

A cozy drink for coffee lovers who want warmth and froth in one.

Steps

  1. Add the whipped coffee to hot milk
  2. Stir and enjoy your hot, fluffy blend
  3. Repeat for a second serving!

Chocolate Coffee Smoothie

A dessert-style cold coffee shake with a chocolate twist.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate syrup in glass
  • 5 ice cubes
  • Cold milk
  • Whipped coffee topping

Instructions

  1. Line glass with chocolate syrup
  2. Add ice and milk
  3. Top with whipped coffee

Smoothie Tips

  • Use chilled or frozen milk for a better texture
  • Add a scoop of protein powder for an energizing boost
  • Sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon for extra flavor
  • Adjust sweetness by varying the sugar or syrup used
