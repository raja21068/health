Oreo Chocolate Smoothie

A cookies-and-cream style smoothie with the richness of chocolate syrup and crunchy Oreos.

Ingredients

  • 5 Oreo biscuits
  • 2 tsb chocolate syrup
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 150ml cold milk

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy
  3. Serve chilled

Creamy Oreo Shake

A thicker version perfect as a dessert, topped with extra cream and ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 80ml milk
  • 4 Oreo biscuits
  • 150g vanilla ice cream
  • Extra cream for topping

Instructions

  1. Blend Oreos and milk until smooth
  2. Add ice cream and blend briefly
  3. Top with cream and serve

Smoothie Tips

  • Use cold milk or add ice cream for a dessert-like texture
  • Adjust sweetness with more or less chocolate syrup
  • Blend with a banana for a thicker, creamier base
  • Top with whipped cream and cocoa powder for presentation
