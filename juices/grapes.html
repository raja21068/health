Fresh Grape Juice Recipe

Sweet, juicy grapes make for a refreshing and vibrant juice. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and natural sweetness, grape juice is a healthy and energizing drink for any occasion.


This easy recipe is quick, refreshing, and perfect for both kids and adults.

Simple Grape Juice

Just a few ingredients for a refreshing homemade juice.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh grapes (red, black, or green)
  • 1 cup cold water (or sparkling water for fizz)
  • 1â€“2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the grapes thoroughly.
  2. Add grapes and water to a blender, blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve or cheesecloth.
  4. Stir in sugar/honey and lemon juice if desired.
  5. Serve chilled over ice cubes and enjoy!

Health Benefits

Grape Juice Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants like resveratrol
  • Supports heart and brain health
  • Hydrating and vitamin-rich (C & K)
  • Natural boost of quick energy

Tips for Best Juice

  • Use seedless grapes for smoother juice
  • Chill or freeze grapes before blending for extra freshness
  • Add a squeeze of lemon for tanginess
  • Mix with apples or berries for unique flavors
