Healthy Screen Time Usage Guide

Explore ways to balance digital connection with healthy habits.

For Adults

  • 2 to 3 hours per day: A moderate amount that includes essential tasks and limited entertainment.
  • Reduce to under 1 hour before bed to improve sleep quality and avoid late-night screen disruptions.

For Teens and Kids

  • 1 to 2 hours of recreational screen time is a healthy limit, especially outside of schoolwork.
  • Excessive time on devices can lead to reduced attention span and sleep issues.
