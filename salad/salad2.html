Salad Recipe Collection

Refreshing global & Asian salads you’ll love

1 Russian Salad (Olivier Salad)

  • Ingredients: Boiled potatoes, carrots, peas, diced apples, boiled eggs, mayo, salt, and pepper.
  • Chop boiled potatoes, carrots, and eggs into cubes.
  • Mix with peas, apples, and mayonnaise.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Chill before serving.
A creamy & classic salad, perfect for parties.

2 Asian Sesame Salad

  • Ingredients: Lettuce, shredded cabbage, carrots, cucumbers, sesame seeds, soy sauce, sesame oil, lime juice.
  • Chop all vegetables thinly.
  • Whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, and lime juice for dressing.
  • Toss veggies with dressing & sprinkle sesame seeds.
Crisp, tangy & nutty – a light Asian favorite!

3 Thai Green Papaya Salad (Som Tam)

  • Ingredients: Shredded green papaya, cherry tomatoes, green beans, chili, garlic, lime juice, fish sauce, peanuts.
  • Pound garlic & chili in mortar for paste.
  • Add lime juice, fish sauce, and palm sugar.
  • Mix with shredded papaya, beans, and tomatoes. Garnish with peanuts.
A spicy, tangy Thai street food classic.

4 Greek Salad

  • Ingredients: Cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, olives, feta cheese, oregano, olive oil.
  • Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion into chunks.
  • Mix with olives and olive oil.
  • Top with feta cubes & sprinkle oregano.
A Mediterranean favorite – fresh & zesty!
